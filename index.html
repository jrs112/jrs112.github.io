About Me

Welcome to my Website! My name is John Ryan Sander and I'm a programmer in training at the University of North Carolina Charlotte Coding Bootcamp. I grew up in Charlotte, North Carolina and then went to attain a Bachelor of Arts in Political Science at Randolph Macon College in Ashland, Virginia.

After graduating college, I did financial sales with New York Life where I was able to gain great experience in both marketing and sales. I then moved back to Charlotte and worked as an Account Manager with Flores and Associates where I was able to develop excellent customer service and client relationship skills. These past experiences allowed me to develop excellent communication skills that will be valuable as I pursue my dream of becoming a web developer.

The University of North Carolina Charlotte Coding Bootcamp is teaching me a full stack web development skillset that will prepare me to become a web developer. The full stack web development skillset that I'm learning includes:

  • HTML/CSS
  • JavaScript
  • asp.Net
  • Bootstrap
  • Node.js

I will also be putting together a portfolio from all of the projects that I complete during the Bootcamp. This portfolio will demonstrate the skillset that I develop during the Bootcamp and show potential employers what I am capable of. You can view my current portfolio by clicking the "Portfolio" link towards the top right of the screen.
