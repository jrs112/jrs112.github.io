About Me

My name is John Ryan Sander and I'm currently in a Web Development program at the University of North Carolina Charlotte to develop the skillset of a full stack Web Developer. I'm a native of Charlotte, NC and have a bachelors degree from Randolph Macon College in Ashland, VA.

After attaining my degree, I stayed in Virginia and worked at New York Life to sell a variety of financial products including insurance and annuities. During my time at New York Life, I was able to gain great experience in both marketing and sales, which has prepared me to develop powerful web applications for businesses.

My business experience continued after New York life, when I was hired by Flores and Associates for an Account Manager position. As an Account Manager, I developed excellent communication skills evident by the world class customer service I provided yielding zero complaints, while fielding hundreds of customer inquiries per week. I'm able to use these communication skills to work effictively with teammates and clients when building web applications.

The University of North Carolina Charlotte Web Development Program is teaching me a full stack web development skillset. The full stack web development skillset that I'm learning includes:

  • HTML/CSS
  • JavaScript
  • asp.Net
  • MySQL
  • Node.js
